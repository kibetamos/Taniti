White Sand Beaches

About

Taniti’s beaches are world-famous for their crystal-clear turquoise waters, soft white sand, and tranquil atmosphere. Whether you’re sunbathing, swimming, or strolling along the shore at sunset, the beaches offer an unforgettable escape.

What to Do

  • Snorkeling and scuba diving in coral reefs
  • Relaxing in seaside cabanas
  • Enjoying local seafood and cocktails at beach bars

Best Time to Visit

The dry season (May–October) offers perfect weather, calm seas, and fewer crowds.
