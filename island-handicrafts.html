Island Handicrafts

About

Taniti’s artisans are masters of traditional crafts passed down through generations. Each handmade item tells a story of culture, community, and creativity.

What to Discover

  • Handwoven baskets and mats made from palm fibers
  • Wood carvings inspired by island myths
  • Locally crafted jewelry using shells and volcanic stones

Where to Buy

Visit the Artisan Market in Taniti Town for authentic souvenirs, or explore small village workshops where you can watch artisans at work.
