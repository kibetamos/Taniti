Volcano Tours

About

Taniti’s active volcano offers a once-in-a-lifetime adventure for nature enthusiasts. The guided tours take you through lava fields, scenic viewpoints, and safe observation decks near the crater.

Highlights

  • Guided hikes with safety briefings
  • Panoramic views of the island from the summit
  • Evening lava glow experiences

Safety & Tips

Always follow your guide’s instructions. Wear sturdy shoes, bring water, and avoid restricted areas near active vents.
