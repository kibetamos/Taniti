Local Cuisine

About

Taniti’s cuisine is a flavorful fusion of traditional island recipes and modern coastal dining. Fresh seafood, tropical fruits, and local spices create a rich culinary experience that reflects the island’s vibrant culture.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Grilled mahi-mahi with coconut rice
  • Banana leaf-wrapped pork roast
  • Tropical fruit platter with mango, pineapple, and passionfruit

Dining Experiences

Enjoy open-air beach restaurants, local food markets, and family-run eateries that offer a taste of authentic island hospitality.
