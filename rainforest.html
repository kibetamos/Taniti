Rainforest Adventures

About

Taniti’s lush rainforests are home to diverse wildlife, hidden waterfalls, and towering trees that stretch to the sky. Adventure-seekers can explore the island’s natural beauty through eco-tours and guided hikes.

Activities

  • Zip-lining over the forest canopy
  • Hiking to secluded waterfalls
  • Wildlife photography and birdwatching

Travel Tips

Wear comfortable hiking shoes and carry insect repellent. Local guides can help you discover secret spots and share Taniti’s fascinating ecology.
