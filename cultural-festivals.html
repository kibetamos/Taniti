Cultural Festivals

About

Throughout the year, Taniti comes alive with colorful cultural festivals celebrating its music, dance, and traditional arts. These gatherings bring locals and visitors together in a joyful display of island pride.

Major Events

  • Taniti Heritage Week: A full week of parades, traditional dances, and food fairs.
  • Ocean Blessing Festival: Honoring the sea and its bounty with songs and offerings.
  • Island Music Nights: Local performers showcasing rhythmic drumming and ukulele tunes.

Visitor Tips

Join the locals, wear colorful attire, and don’t miss the nightly performances at the central plaza!
